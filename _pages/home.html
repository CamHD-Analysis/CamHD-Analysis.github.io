Cloud-Capable Tools for CamHD Data Analysis

This is the public website for the NSF OTIC-funded project "Cloud-Capable Tools for CamHD Data Analysis," a collaborative effort between Tim Crone at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory and Aaron Marburg at University of Washington Applied Physics Laboratory.

Our goal is to engage with the data being gathered by the high-definition camera installed on the NSF-funded Ocean Observatories Initiative (OOI) Cabled Array installed on Axial volcano off the Oregon Coast. We are working on tools to produce quantitative data from the video, and to develop tools / best practices for handling the large (~1 GB/minute) uncompressed video files.
